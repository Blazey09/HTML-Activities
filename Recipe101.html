Recipe for Karage Don

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tenders
  • Oil
  • Egg
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder

Procedure

  1. Season chicken tenders with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder and soy sauce.
  2. Leave the chicken tenders to rest for about 15 minutes.
  3. Break the eggs and mix thoroughly.
  4. Coat the chicken tenders with the egg batter.
  5. After the chicken tenders are coated with the egg batter cover it with bread crumbs. Spread evenly and shake any excess crumbs.
  6. Deep fry the coated chicken tenders with olive oil in medium high heat until crisp and golden brown.
  7. Serve Hot and Enjoy!
